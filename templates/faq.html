1, How many Capri points do I pay for one fuel-up?

We deduct 5 points/gallon from your Capri account for each fuel-up. For example we will take 50 points for 50 gallons of gas.

2, What reward can I expect?

We give out on average 5¢ per gallon for each fuel-up. However it is possible that you can win more (or less) than that by trying out your luck in the ticket tape game.
